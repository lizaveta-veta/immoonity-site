What happens to immunity in space?

Microgravity, radiation, and stress disrupt the immune system — often before symptoms appear

T‑cells
Reduced activation — the immune system's "soldiers" become slower and less effective at fighting infections.
Inflammation
Chronic low‑grade inflammation rises — the immune system stays in "alert mode," draining resources.
Viruses
Latent herpes viruses (EBV, VZV, HSV‑1) reactivate in up to 65% of crew members — a clear sign of immune dysregulation.
